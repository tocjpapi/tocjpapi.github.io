Elanique—(03);

(24’).

Web Design & Development

‘Elanique’ is a Design and Development exploration for a fictional design agency that was used to hone my skills.

Alignment Break.

Problem;

The aim for ‘Elanique’ was to make a minimalist, conventional website that obeys design principles but at the same time tests their limits in a rather unconventional way. Subtly pushing the boundaries of a classic—purist form of design hence making the most use of the raw materials.

Role;

As the web designer and developer, my role was to create a memorable experience whilst at the same time not being simply ‘way too much’ for the user. It was to craft a lovely interface without being overbearing.

Framed Shots;

A rather lovely collection containing and exploring my best shots for the project. It’s basically a display of the parts I could very often look back on and say, “I’m proud of this.”

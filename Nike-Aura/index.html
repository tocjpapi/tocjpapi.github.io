Nike Aura—(04);

(24’).

Web Prototyping & Motion

‘Nike Aura’ is a Prototyping and Motion exploration for a hypothesis I had on nike’s photography.

Alignment Break.

Problem;

If ‘Nike’ were to make a photography portfolio, how will it be? Those are the questions I asked myself. I wanted to explore the idea of a photography portfolio for Nike, and how it would look like if they were to make one. I wanted to see how I could use their brand identity to create a bespoke experience.

Role;

I decided on taking the unconventional route to the motion design and prototyping of this website. Featuring a dynamic navigation machanism, an aggressive parallax deeply inspired by ‘Legend Studio’ by Aristide Benoist. Altogether yeilding to a lovely project and a wonderful learning experience.

Framed Shots;

A rather lovely collection containing and exploring my best shots for the project. It’s basically a display of the parts I could very often look back on and say, “I’m proud of this.”

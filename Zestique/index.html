(25’).

Web Design & Development

‘Zestique’ is an ecommerce website for an Instagram brand aiming to blur the line between form and function.

Alignment Break.

Problem;

The stakeholders behind Zestique envisioned more than just a website; they sought an experience. Something beautiful yet functional. Unconventional yet intuitive. A space that doesn't just display products but evokes emotion, making users pause, admire, and effortlessly convert admiration into action.

Role;

I was the web designer and developer, I figured the answer was not just to build a website but to craft an experience. Every element had to feel intentional, every interaction had to be seamless. The design would embrace bold aesthetics without sacrificing clarity, guiding users effortlessly from curiosity to conversion.
